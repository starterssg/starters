Starters Singapore

Starters is a Singapore-based social club
that brings two groups of friends,
three ladies and three gents,
together over food & drinks.

We are a social club that brings two groups of awesome people together. Meetings are set up between three boys and three girls, just to spice things up! ;)

All you need to do is sign up with Facebook, and invite two of your friends to join your group. Based on your info, we'll match you up with a group we just know you'll hit it off with, hosted at the most happening bars in Singapore.

We organize the place, the drinks, and of course, the other three. All you have to do is reserve your slot and pay for your first round of drinks.

How does it work?

Step One

Sign up on our main page. You will be prompted to access your Facebook account so that we have a better profile of you.


Tip: We use Facebook login so we can match groups easier without making you fill out piles and piles of forms. We will NEVER post on your wall, show up in your profile in any way, or post your embarassing photos to your exes.

Step Two

You will receive an e-mail from us with a link to invite your buddies.


Tip: You MUST bring two friends in order to register your group! Trust us, everything is always more fun when you do it with friends!

Step Three

When a matching group is found, we will notify you and confirm a convenient time for everyone. We'll send you an email with the time, venue, and a free drinks voucher. All you need to do is show up and have fun!

Tip: Please be patient if this takes time. We would rather make you wait a little longer to find a good fit than setting you up with the wrong crowd. If you are impatient, you can speed up the process by inviting more friends!

We make Singapore a more happening place.
